The Archive Collection

Rarity Tiers

Common

Core archive planets. Strong visual worlds with standard system traits.

Uncommon

Planets with stronger visual signatures, extra moons, or more interesting science traits.

Rare

Ring systems, multi-star systems, unusual discovery paths, or high-impact world types.

Epic

Highly distinctive worlds with special background treatment, major system features, or elevated rarity scores.

Legendary

Elite archive entries. These should feel like major Syndicate discoveries or mission-critical worlds.

Mythic

Reserved for one-of-one story planets, founder pieces, or major lore artifacts.
