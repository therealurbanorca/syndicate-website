About

A fictional archive built from real cosmic inspiration.

The Syndicate Exoplanet Archive is a science-fiction NFT project built around real exoplanet discovery and transformed into cinematic collector artwork.

Each preview world connects visual traits, rarity systems, moons, rings, science-rooted language, and lore from The Syndicate universe. The NFT collection is the launch focus. The novel and merch will expand the universe after the archive has its foundation.
