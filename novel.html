The Syndicate Novel

The story behind the archive.

The NFT collection is the launch focus. The novel expands the universe behind the archive and gives the planets a deeper story foundation.

Synopsis

Peter Sterling was six years old when his parents were murdered in a storm-soaked ambush that should have killed him too. Instead, he survived through experimental alien-derived medicine, altered blood, and a secret his guardians buried for sixteen years.

Now twenty-two, Peter is recruited into the Syndicate, a hidden organization operating far beyond the limits of public science. Trained as a covert operative inside what he believes is an underground facility, Peter quickly realizes the Syndicate is watching him, testing him, and hiding the true scale of what it has built.

Then a training accident shatters the illusion.

The facility is not underground. It is in orbit.

As Peter uncovers the truth about his parents, his impossible abilities, the sentient AI known as Max, and the classified Exoplanet Archive, a masked enemy called the Baron begins moving against the Syndicate from the shadows. His soldiers wear self-destructing exosuits. His army is built from failed recruits, stolen technology, and old blood feuds.

To save the people he loves and stop a war hidden from Earth, Peter must stop hiding what he is.

He must awaken.
