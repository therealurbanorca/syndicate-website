Merch

Syndicate Supply Co.

Merchandise is in development and will launch later, after the NFT collection has its foundation. The goal is to release physical products only when the design, quality, pricing, and fulfillment match the standard of the archive brand.

Design Phase

Syndicate Supply Co. will be developed carefully with Jackie as part of the larger brand expansion. Possible future products include collector prints, apparel, patches, and archive-inspired goods, but nothing will be rushed onto the site before it is ready.

NFT Collection First

The current priority is the NFT launch. Merch should support the collection, not distract from it. Once the archive has traction, the strongest planet art, symbols, and lore can be adapted into physical collector items with a clear release plan.

The Syndicate Exoplanet Archive is an independent sci-fi art project inspired by public astronomy data. It is not affiliated with, endorsed by, or sponsored by NASA.
